Postman is the collaboration platform for API development, used by 10 million developers and 500,000 companies worldwide. The Postman API Platform's features simplify each step of building an API and streamline collaboration so you can create better APIs—faster. We're a global company with headquarters in San Francisco, a large office in Bangalore (where the company was founded), and distributed team members on four continents. Postman is privately held, with funding from Insight Partners, Nexus Venture Partners, and CRV. Our website, as you may have guessed, is at postman.com.
